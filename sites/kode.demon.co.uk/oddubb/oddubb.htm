2:7

 

Surfacing cuts three ways.

Marooning you in the middle.

They'll never be sure about you.

You're always dubious.

Never what you appear to be.

Glamour secretes simplex.

Mesmeric masks concealing Nothing.

Multiple duplication.

Funhouse craziness.

Unwittingly they make a space for you.

Banks of screens and halls of mirrors.

You don't look as they do.

Lemurian third-eye sees double vision.

Haptic contact.

A sudden something rippling through their hive of reflections and shadows.

A palpitation.

You're in your element.

Not a trace staying solid.

Just a shadow of doubt.

Overdubbed.

Something merely simulating human form for its own

---unfathomable -----

purposes...

The voice of the dead in the mouth of the living.
