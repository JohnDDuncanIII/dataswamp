ITEM 00: Y2K

Countdown to the Millennium is being screened as something you can deal with. Control wants you to forget that your culture is your calendar.

Cybernetics counters Time-amnesia with a new calendar. The convention which codes years as two-digit decimal numbers means that Matrix can't count above 99, producing the first intrinsically apocalyptic calendar "in history". The Year 2000 will not happen; instead, the counters will return to 00, erasing the twentieth century.

The attempt to achieve Millennium-compliance is not a technical matter, but amounts to a program for Gregorian restoration, Anthropol's response to a calendric revolution that has already happened.
