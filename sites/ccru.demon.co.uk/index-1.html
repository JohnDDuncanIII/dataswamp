Ccru is a cultural production and experimentation collective.

 

Ccru has no genealogy, geographical centre, biographical attribution or institutional dependency.

 

Ccru activity involves dehumanization of the voice/destratification of sound, proliferation of fictional quantities, anomalous theory, and image contagion.

 

Ccru consists of Datable Swarm-Convergences in process. Its emergence is sequenced and accelerated by a series of singularities (Barker-Thresholds):

the Virtual Futures conferences (Spring K-094, K-095, K-096), the O[rphan] D[rift] Cyberpositive book (K-095), the ***collapse journal (K-095-K-096), the Afro-Futures event (February K-096), the ongoing Ko:Labs breakbeat experimentation zone, the Virotechnics event (October K-097), the Abstract Culture pamphlet series (K097 +), the Ccru/O[rphan] D[rift] collaboration (Beaconsfield Arts Centre, London, Autumn K-098)

 

Ccru defines Cybernetic Culture immanently, as the mode of propogation characterizing flat productive collectivities. Such flatness--whose intensive Quanta are Ccrunits or Barkers--involves:

1) Coincidence of product-process

2) Counter-chronic arrival (from machinic virtuality)

3) Absolute impersonality, ahistoricity, and extraterritoriality.
