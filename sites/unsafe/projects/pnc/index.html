Edit23 is to be a new kind of text editor - a graphical text editor, with snazzy features such as a document browse function. This function will enable you to jump to the next (or previous) textfile in your current folder by a simple command-page up or page down click.

Right now Edit23 is just an idea that might become a Java executable in the future.

The "eye in the triangle" icon will become a directional menu. By clicking and dragging either right/left/up/down, you can access the items on the menu. Try it!
