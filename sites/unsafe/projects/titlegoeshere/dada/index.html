New innovations in the instruments themselves have led also to a whole new world of music. Now Martin Denny plays the keyboard of a Moog synthesizer and records his music on an 8-track recording apparatus that can solo out any instrument in the band individually, or blend them together with extreme precision. The synthesizer itself is probably the most unique instrument to ever be invented. It resembles a telephone switchboard in appearance, yet can imitate any instrument in the orchestra - it is the daddy of electronics in music.
