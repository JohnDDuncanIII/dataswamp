game design and consultancy

 
Skin-Crawlers
Guide Zeta Kane as she pursues the techno-horror mystery of the worm through the hazardous world of the Cybergothic near-future. Features implant enhancements, Artificial-Death, Tzikvik sorcery and a self-assembling psychopathic hyper-intelligence.

Cryptolith
2012 Theta station Antarctic Peninsula. You are the sole surviving member of a search team sent to investigate the outbreak of a strange infection within an AI controlled secret research base. Hack your way past the security codes of the "Asimov golem", fight a hostile population of artificial "hominids," and explore the shuddering nightmare of a lost civilization buried in the catacombs beneath the Antarctic ice.

Assault on the Aquapolis
You are Cargo-27, a black Atlantean aquassassin. Using stealth, bodytech and combat skills you infiltrate the sinister undersea ‘Bubble-Pod' in an attempt to uncover and sabotage the abominable bio-mutation experiments of mad plutocrat Max Crabbe.

Kataklysm
Occult quest to the heart of darkness. The year is 1883. Rogue adventurer Cecil Curtis is sent by the enigmatic Architectonic Order of the Eschaton in search of the Limbic Key. This mission leads through the steaming jungles of eastern Sumatra deep into the territory of ferocious Tak Nma head-hunters, where the demon Katak prowls through malarial dreams, and the rumbling of Krakatoa grows ever louder...

Time-Wars
Secret societies and techno-sorcerous street cults wage a multi-level cosmic battle over the destiny of cyberspace, escalating across warped immensities of space and time.

Lemuria
Overcome powerful adversaries to build a biotechnical culture across the drifting continents of pre-cretacean Earth.

Howl of the Wendigo
Play with the dead at Joe Wendigo's cursed casino, built upon a an ancient Tzikvik burial ground, and haunted by electronic ghosts.
